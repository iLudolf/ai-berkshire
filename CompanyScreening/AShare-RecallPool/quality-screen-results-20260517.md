## Key Findings

1. **Food & Beverage and Home Appliances are A-shares' two highest-quality sectors** (90–100% pass rate); sector leaders consistently show high gross margins + strong free cash flow + zero dilution
2. **New Energy and High-End Manufacturing & Defense had the highest elimination rates** (50–60% eliminated), primarily due to cyclical sector downturns and defense procurement pricing constraints
3. **Among the 7 criteria, ②FCF negative and ①ROE<8% were the most lethal**, eliminating 15 and 12 companies respectively
4. **Exemption rules effectively prevented false eliminations**: BYD, Foxconn Industrial Internet, Luxshare Precision, S.F. Holding, and other high-turnover/thin-margin companies were retained via Exemption C
