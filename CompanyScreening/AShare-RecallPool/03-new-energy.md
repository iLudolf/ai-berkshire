## 1. CATL (300750)

**Market cap**: CNY 2.1tn | **PE(TTM)**: 25x

### Core Investment Thesis

Global power battery leader with 37%+ market share; scale effects and technology iteration speed far outpace competitors. 2025 net income CNY 72.2bn (+42%); energy storage has become a second growth curve, raising the long-term ceiling.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Technology moat is extremely deep: Kirin Battery, Shenxing ultra-fast charging, and condensed-state batteries continuously lead the industry. Scale moat: #1 global production capacity, clear cost advantage. Customer lock-in: ties with Tesla, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, and other global mainstream automakers — switching costs are very high. Brand effects earn a premium in energy storage procurement tenders.

### Valuation Assessment

25x PE against 42% profit growth makes PEG < 1 appear reasonable. However, a CNY 2.1tn market cap means growth rates must inevitably slow — the key is judging the long-term steady-state profit level.

**Conclusion**: Warrants deep research. The key question at a CNY 2tn+ market cap is whether growth can persist — specifically, whether energy storage can generate a "power-battery-scale" profit pool.

## 2. BYD (002594)

**Market cap**: CNY 1tn | **PE(TTM)**: 23x

### Core Investment Thesis

Global NEV sales champion (4.27mn units); vertical integration model (battery + chips + full vehicle) builds extremely strong cost control. Globalisation accelerating with overseas revenue at 38.7% — replicating the domestic success story internationally.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Vertical integration is the core barrier: from minerals to batteries to finished vehicles, full in-house R&D and manufacturing, with unmatched cost control. Scale effects combined with fast technology iteration make it difficult for competitors to catch up. Brand is breaking upmarket (Yangwang, Denza) with a product matrix covering the CNY 50,000–1,000,000 price range. However, the auto industry is inherently highly competitive; the moat width is narrower than consumer goods.

### Valuation Assessment

23x PE is reasonable against high growth, but the ongoing auto price war makes profit margin sustainability the key variable. If net margin stabilises at 5%+, the current valuation is attractive.

## 3. Sungrow (300274)

**Market cap**: CNY 285bn | **PE(TTM)**: 21x

### Core Investment Thesis

Global inverter leader + energy storage as the first major business segment (CNY 37.3bn / +49%); dual-engine driven. Overseas revenue exceeds 60%, benefiting from high overseas margins. Integrated solar-plus-storage solution capability builds differentiated competitive strength.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Inverter technical barriers + brand recognition + global channel network create competitive advantages. Energy storage system integration capability is globally leading, with rich experience in large-scale ground-mounted projects. However, hardware products ultimately face pricing competition and moat width is limited. 2026Q1 profit -40% exposed earnings volatility risk.

### Valuation Assessment

21x PE appears reasonable against high growth, but the Q1 profit collapse indicates insufficient earnings stability. Need to observe whether H2 can recover; if full-year profit still grows, the current level is a buying point.

## 4. Inovance Technology (300124)

**Market cap**: CNY 180bn | **Static PE**: 42x | **2026E PE**: 26x

### Core Investment Thesis

"China's Siemens" — core beneficiary of domestic substitution in industrial controls. Three overlapping growth theses: general automation + NEV electric drive + humanoid robots. ROE consistently above 20%; management execution is extremely strong.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Technical barriers: self-developed servo systems, variable frequency drives, PLCs, and other core industrial control products that match or exceed foreign brand performance. High customer stickiness: industrial control products, once embedded in production lines, have very high switching costs. R&D driven: 10%+ R&D spending rate with continuous category expansion. Humanoid robots represent a long-dated option. Against Siemens and ABB, global expansion remains a weakness.

### Valuation Assessment

Static 42x PE is expensive; if 2026 profit materialises, 26x is reasonable. Core risk is that valuation already prices in high expectations, leaving little room for error.

## 5. Sanhua Intelligent Controls (002050)

**Market cap**: CNY 180bn | **PE(TTM)**: 37x

### Core Investment Thesis

Thermal management hidden champion with 20%+ global market share. Simultaneously benefiting from three major segments: refrigeration/HVAC + NEV thermal management + data centre liquid cooling — one of the few cross-industry platform-type component companies.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Absolute leader in its niche: electronic expansion valve global market share exceeds 50%; technical barriers + scale effects create a deep moat. Customers span global mainstream automakers and HVAC companies; switching costs are high. Data centre liquid cooling is a new growth driver, benefiting from the AI compute boom. Products appear simple but require extremely precise manufacturing processes, making entry by latecomers difficult.

### Valuation Assessment

37x PE is clearly expensive; the market has fully priced in its growth potential. Requires valuation digestion or an earnings beat to create a margin of safety.
