## 1. Kingsoft Office (688111.SH)

**Market cap**: ~CNY 115bn | **PE(TTM)**: ~50x

### Core Investment Thesis

Absolute leader in domestic productivity software; 672mn monthly active users form a data flywheel. AI transformation is accelerating (WPS AI MAU 80mn, +307% YoY); Xinchuang (IT localisation) policy drives government-enterprise substitution. Business model is upgrading from "selling licences" to "selling AI services."

### Economic Moat Analysis

Multi-layer moat stacked — user scale and habit barrier (672mn MAU); policy barrier in government-enterprise Xinchuang (virtually irreplaceable in the government market); switching costs from 30 years of document technology accumulation; and the data barrier in the AI era.

### Valuation Assessment

Expensive. 50x PE is still demanding even considering high AI growth expectations — unless AI paid conversion rates rise rapidly.

## 2. China Mobile (600941.SH)

**Market cap**: ~CNY 2,000bn | **PE(TTM)**: ~15x | **Dividend yield**: ~4–5%

### Core Investment Thesis

China's largest telecom operator; near-monopoly user base (1bn+ users) and network infrastructure. Transitioning from "pipe provider" to "communications + technology"; government-enterprise business and AI compute are becoming second growth curves. High dividends + low valuation delivers steady returns.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Extremely wide moat — licence monopoly + infrastructure scale effects + 1bn user base form a triple barrier. The three-oligopoly structure in telecom is stable; price war probability is low. The enormous investment in 5G/compute networks further raises entry barriers.

### Valuation Assessment

Cheap. 15x PE is fair-to-cheap for a global telecom leader; 4–5% dividend yield is highly attractive.

## 3. S.F. Holding (002352.SZ)

**Market cap**: ~CNY 190–200bn | **PE(TTM)**: ~16x

### Core Investment Thesis

Absolute leader in China's mid-to-high-end express delivery, with 60%+ time-sensitive shipment market share. International expansion (Kerry Logistics integration, Ezhou Huahu Airport commissioned) opens global-scale possibilities. 2026E profit CNY 12bn+.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Brand recognition is the core moat — "send important documents via SF" is already embedded in users' minds. Self-built air + ground network creates a heavy-asset barrier. However, express delivery competition is fierce; the Tongda network (ZTO, YTO etc.) applies continuous pricing pressure in the lower segments; moat width is limited.

### Valuation Assessment

Reasonable. 16x PE against CNY 12bn profit — current pricing is approximately fair.

## 4. Glodon (002410.SZ)

**Market cap**: ~CNY 23bn | **PE(TTM)**: ~55–60x

### Core Investment Thesis

Absolute leader in construction informatisation; 70%+ market share in construction cost software. SaaS cloud transition is entering the harvest phase. AI + BIM may open a new growth dimension, but construction industry cyclicality is the key variable.

## 5. Focus Media (002027.SZ)

**Market cap**: ~CNY 88–107bn | **PE(TTM)**: ~13–17x

### Core Investment Thesis

Absolute leader in elevator media (64% market share); the "high-reach + high-frequency + high-completion" forced-exposure scenario is irreplaceable. Asset-light model delivers extremely high ROE (30%+); cash flows are excellent. Significant earnings leverage in advertising market recovery cycles.

### Economic Moat Analysis

First-mover advantage + scale effects form the moat — premium-location resources in core cities are scarce and most are locked up by Focus Media through long-term leases; new entrants cannot secure an equivalent network of locations. However, business structure is highly concentrated; performance is heavily dependent on the macroeconomic cycle.

### Valuation Assessment

Cheap-to-reasonable. 13–17x PE is clearly cheap for an asset-light, high-ROE model.

## 6. Hundsun Technologies (600570.SH)

**Market cap**: ~CNY 49bn | **PE(TTM)**: ~35x

### Core Investment Thesis

Absolute leader in China's financial IT sector; core product lines hold 50–90% market share. Financial institution digital transformation is a long-term trend; AI empowering investment research and trading opens new growth potential. On the FinTech100 global list for 15 consecutive years.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Extremely strong customer stickiness is the core moat — securities trading systems 50% market share; fund investment trading 83%; bank wealth management 80%; trust asset management 90%. Financial institutions' extreme requirements for system "stability + security" make switching costs extremely high.

### Valuation Assessment

Fair-to-slightly-expensive. 35x PE for a financial IT leader premium is understandable, but profit growth rate support is needed.
