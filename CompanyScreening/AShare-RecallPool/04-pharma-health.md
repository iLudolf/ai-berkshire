## 1. Hengrui Medicine (600276)

**Market cap**: CNY 368bn | **PE(TTM)**: 48x | **2026E PE**: 39x

### Core Investment Thesis

Absolute leader in A-share innovative drugs; innovative drug revenue of CNY 16.3bn represents 58% of total — transformation successful. Pipeline is extremely rich, covering oncology, autoimmune, metabolic, and other hot targets; multiple blockbuster candidates entering the harvest phase over the next three years.

### Economic Moat Analysis

R&D platform company simultaneously advancing 60+ clinical programmes; pipeline depth and breadth are unrivalled domestically. Nationwide sales network with strong academic promotion capability. However, innovative drug competition is fierce; single-product moats are limited, and the company relies on continuous innovation. Management's R&D commitment is resolute; since Sun Piaoyong's return, the strategy has been clear.

### Valuation Assessment

48x PE is expensive for an innovative drug leader; the market has priced in a certainty premium. Unless a blockbuster product significantly exceeds expectations, the current price lacks a margin of safety.

## 2. Mindray Medical (300760)

**Market cap**: CNY 216bn | **PE(TTM)**: 27x | **2026E PE**: 21x

### Core Investment Thesis

Absolute leader in domestic medical devices, ranked in the global top 20. Products span three major areas: patient monitoring & life support, in-vitro diagnostics, and medical imaging. 2025 revenue -9.4% from anti-corruption impact, but overseas +7.4% validates global competitiveness. Earnings trough + reasonable valuation is the classic "great company meeting short-term difficulty."

### Economic Moat Analysis

Extremely broad product line: three business segments reinforce each other, with strong bundled selling capability. Technical barriers: high-end patient monitors and ultrasound products approach the performance of the "GPS" trio (GE / Philips / Siemens). Global channel: present in 190+ countries with continuously improving brand recognition. Medical device stickiness is extreme — hospitals rarely switch once they procure a system. 10%+ R&D spending rate ensures continuous innovation.

### Valuation Assessment

27x PE is at the low end of historical range; if 2026 earnings recover to growth, 21x PE is highly attractive. Anti-corruption impact is one-time and does not alter the long-term thesis.

## 3. WuXi AppTec (603259)

**Market cap**: CNY 300bn | **PE(TTM)**: 16x

### Core Investment Thesis

Global CXO leader; backlog of CNY 59.9bn (+41%) demonstrates robust customer demand. Valuation has been suppressed by the US Biosecurity Act; at 16x PE against the irreversible trend of global drug R&D outsourcing, this is a pricing error created by policy panic.

### Economic Moat Analysis

The fully integrated CRDMO platform is globally unique — one-stop service from drug discovery to commercial manufacturing. Customer stickiness is extremely high: once a drug enters clinical development, switching CXO suppliers requires re-filing, at extreme cost. 19 of the world's top 20 pharma companies are customers. Chemical synthesis capability is world-class; production scale is unmatched. Risk: geopolitical uncertainty.

### Valuation Assessment

16x PE is at historical absolute lows; clearly undervalued even accounting for a geopolitical risk discount. High backlog growth demonstrates that actual business is unaffected — the market is overreacting to policy risk.

## 4. Pien Tze Huang (600436)

**Market cap**: CNY 103bn | **PE(TTM)**: 45x

### Core Investment Thesis

State-classified formula + exclusive access to natural musk — the moat depth may be the deepest of any A-share company. Brand premium is extremely strong, with continuously proven pricing power through successive price increases. However, 2025 saw the first double decline (both revenue and profit) — the key question is whether this is cyclical or structural.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Uniquely unassailable moat: the state-classified formula cannot be replicated; natural musk sourcing is government-controlled and allocated exclusively to Pien Tze Huang. Brand history exceeds 500 years; known as the "pharmaceutical Moutai" in the Southeast Asian Chinese diaspora. The product has no substitutes; demand is highly inelastic. The only risk is that natural musk supply constraints limit production expansion.

### Valuation Assessment

45x PE is expensive for a consumer goods company with slowing growth. The moat is deep but growth potential is limited; a larger margin of safety is needed. Ideal entry range: PE below 30x.

## 5. Aier Eye Hospital (300015)

**Market cap**: CNY 100bn | **PE(TTM)**: 31x

### Core Investment Thesis

World's largest ophthalmology chain, with tiered expansion achieved through a franchise-chain model. Growth has slowed to low single digits but valuation has returned to historical lows; myopia prevention and ageing-population cataract demand are long-term certainties.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Network effect: the tiered chain network of 800+ hospitals/clinics globally is difficult to replicate. Brand recognition in ophthalmology is absolute leader; patients first think of Aier when considering eye surgery. The physician training system is mature; talent barriers are evident. However, the chain model faces management-radius challenges, and goodwill risk warrants attention.

### Valuation Assessment

31x PE is at historical lows; if growth can recover to 15%+, it offers good allocation value. Need to monitor 2026 outpatient volume recovery.

## 6. Changchun High & New Technology (000661)

**Market cap**: CNY 52.4bn | **Profit crashed 94%**

### Core Investment Thesis

Absolute leader in growth hormone (Jinsai Pharmaceutical). Profit collapsed 94% after the hit from healthcare insurance price cuts. A high-risk high-return candidate: if growth hormone demand irreversibly declines, value approaches zero; if this is just short-term pain, the current price is deeply undervalued.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Growth hormone technical barriers are high; injectable solution and long-acting formulations are domestically leading. However, healthcare insurance price cuts directly undermined the core profit model — policy has eroded the moat. Indication expansion (adult growth hormone deficiency, assisted reproduction, etc.) represents potential incremental opportunity. Jinsai Pharmaceutical's brand and channels still hold advantages, but policy risk remains unresolved.

### Valuation Assessment

Profit collapse distorts PE — normalised profit estimates are needed. If earnings recover to the CNY 5bn level, the corresponding 10x PE would be highly attractive, but the certainty of recovery is in doubt.

## 7. Hualan Biological (002007)

**Market cap**: CNY 27bn | **PE(TTM)**: 29x | **ROE**: 8.2%

### Core Investment Thesis

Blood products industry has extremely high entry barriers (plasma collection station licence controls); supply is naturally constrained. However, ROE of only 8.2% signals below-average capital efficiency — management's use of capital is mediocre.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Blood products industry entry barriers are extremely high: plasma station licence approvals are strict and new entrants are virtually impossible. Raw material (plasma) scarcity guarantees a supply-deficit structure. However, the company is not the absolute industry leader (Tiantan Biological is larger); product mix and profit-per-litre-of-plasma have room for improvement. Vaccine business is volatile.

### Valuation Assessment

29x PE for 8.2% ROE is not attractive. Unless ROE improves to 15%+, the stock lacks investment appeal.

## 8. Yunnan Baiyao (000538)

**Market cap**: CNY 99bn | **PE(TTM)**: 19x | **ROE**: 13% | **Dividend yield**: ~4.7%

### Core Investment Thesis

Steady-state TCM + consumer goods candidate with extremely strong brand. Operating quality has improved since exiting equity investments to focus on core business; payout ratio raised to 90%. 19x PE + high dividend is suitable as a defensive allocation.

### Economic Moat Analysis

Deep brand moat: Yunnan Baiyao brand has over a century of history and has an irreplaceable consumer mindshare in haemostasis and wound care. Oral care business (toothpaste) holds the #1 domestic market share, with broad channel coverage. TCM formula secrecy (national classified variety) provides product barriers. However, growth potential is limited, and the monetisation ceiling for TCM brands is visible.

### Valuation Assessment

19x PE + 90% payout ratio + 13% ROE offers allocation value as a bond-like asset. Does not pursue high growth; targets steady returns.
