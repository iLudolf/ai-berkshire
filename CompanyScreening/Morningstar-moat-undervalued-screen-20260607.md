## VIII. Usage Notes

1. **Prices need verification**: Data in this report is drawn from Morningstar articles at various points in time; prices may have changed. Before investing, check real-time prices and recalculate upside potential.
2. **Uncertainty rating matters**: High-upside stocks often also have high uncertainty (e.g., HUBS, ADNT). Morningstar's uncertainty rating (Low/Medium/High/Very High) reflects confidence in the fair value estimate.
3. **Limited A-share and HK coverage**: Morningstar covers ~64 A-share companies and limited HK stocks, which is why Chinese stocks are far fewer than US stocks in this report.
4. **Moat downgrade risk**: Adobe and Salesforce were recently downgraded from Wide to Narrow moat (AI competition); similar downgrades could occur at other companies.
5. **Cyclical factors**: Companies like ADNT and CNH at industry cycle troughs — the discount may reflect cyclical rather than permanent impairment.
