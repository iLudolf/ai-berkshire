## Key Findings

1. **Semiconductor equipment is the highest-quality track** — 7 companies passed; the "picks-and-shovels" model is the most robust under quality elimination screening
2. **High gross margin is the common trait of good businesses** — passed companies have a median gross margin of ~45%, vs. ~25% for all 100 companies
3. **Overall earnings quality on the STAR Market is concerning** — 56% of companies were eliminated for being unprofitable; "technology growth" ≠ "good business"
4. **Boundary companies worth long-term tracking** — 18 companies including Hygon Information, Tuojing Technology, and China Railway Signal & Communication require further financial data validation
