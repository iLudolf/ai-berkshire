## I. Nature of the Business

E-commerce platform; an export vehicle for Chinese manufacturing capacity. Business model earns profit through advertising and transaction commissions.

---

## II. Economic Moat

**Two-sided network effect**: More users → more concentrated demand → more merchants, higher switching costs → scale drives down cost → lower prices → attracts more users.

**Extreme efficiency (Costco model)**: Very high revenue-per-employee; focused on e-commerce without spreading thin; unlike Alibaba's undisciplined spending. Revenue growth in recent years confirms this.

**Differentiated experience (Disney model)**: Taobao is primarily search-driven; Pinduoduo is primarily browse-driven, layered with gamification design. High user stickiness, differentiated from Taobao/JD, limited downside.

**Focus**: Unlike Alibaba's sprawl. Concentrated on e-commerce + new Pinmo supply-chain brands + Temu international expansion.

**Fair value range**:
- 10x PE × RMB 100B + RMB 400B cash = **RMB 1.4T** (+40% vs. current)
- 15x PE × RMB 100B + RMB 400B cash = **RMB 1.9T** (+90% vs. current)
- Margin of safety approximately **40–60% discount**.

**Downside protection**: Price-to-book near 1x (market cap almost equals net assets) — extremely rare for an internet platform; essentially a floor.

## V. What If the Thesis Is Wrong

4. **Extreme drop to RMB 500B**: Very low probability — cash alone is RMB 400B; price-to-book = 1x is the floor.
